Section 04

About

Zerm is a local voice-to-clipboard tool for developers. Tap a key, speak, paste. Whisper transcribes on-device using the Apple Silicon GPU. A small local LLM polishes the output. No cloud, no telemetry, no accounts.

Privacy

Everything runs on your machine. The only network call is to your local Ollama instance at localhost:11434. Your microphone audio, raw transcripts, and final outputs never leave the device.
