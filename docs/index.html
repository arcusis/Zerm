Voice to clipboard.
On your machine.

Tap a key, speak, paste. Whisper transcribes on your GPU, a local LLM polishes the text, and it lands in whatever you've got focused. No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry.

Instant

Whisper runs on Apple Silicon's Metal GPU. The typical end-to-end round trip is well under two seconds on a warm pipeline.

Private

Audio, transcripts and outputs never leave the machine. The only network call is to your local Ollama instance on localhost:11434.

Yours to shape

Four prompt modes, custom vocabulary for names Whisper mis-hears, swappable LLM, remappable hotkey, persistent pill position.

How it works

Four keystrokes, zero friction.

  1. 1
    Tap Right Option. A small glass pill appears and starts listening.
  2. 2
    Speak. Up to ~15 minutes, any supported language. Stop talking and Zerm auto-detects the silence.
  3. 3
    Paste. Cleaned text lands in your clipboard and pastes into the focused app automatically.
